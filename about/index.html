My love of photography started in childhood when I would browse though old family photographs, my firm favorites were the traditional black and white or sepia portraits.

As I grew older looking developed into taking, I now have two children of my own watching them grow is magical, with all the different stages of life.

It’s now nearly a decade since photography progressed from a hobby to become my full time profession, but I still enjoy it just as much today as I did when I was a child.

I have never lost my sole objective, to create timeless photographs which will be viewed with the same interest and emotion as I viewed my own family photographs all those years ago.
