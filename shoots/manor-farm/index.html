Paula Jayne Photography often uses the lovely location of Manor Farm.

If you would like to book a session please email paula@paulajayne.com or call 07866 432580.

I love the colours and texture of winter—the light is often softer than summer shoots, the colours richer and children look fresh with rosy coloured cheeks and textured clothing. With the use of hats, scarves and wellies you can create natural cosy images. Manor Farm garden is the perfect location with so much interest, trees, and my favourite rustic doorways. Inside there is a lovely room with a fireplace and window seats for a more posed image.

The full address for the shoot is Manor Farm, Bicester Road, Launton, OX26 5DP. Manor Farm shares the same entrance as the Tythe Barn. To enter Manor Farm continue past the turning for the barn car park, and the drive leads into Manor Farm.
